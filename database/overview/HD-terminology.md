● Function: Communication, manifestation, expression

● Function: Willpower, ego, material world, promises

● Undefined: Inconsistent willpower; tries to prove worth

Sacral Center (Motor Center)

● Undefined: No sustainable work energy; not designed for physical work 
● Chakra Correlation: Sacral chakra (Svādhiṣṭhāna) - split energy

Spleen Center (Awareness Center)

● Undefined: Inconsistent intuition; holds onto what's not healthy
