## WHAT ARE VARIABLES?

Variables are the four arrows in your Human Design chart that represent the deepest cognitive and physical mechanics of how you're designed to operate. They are also called **"The Four Transformations"** and determine:

**Variable emerges from TONE** - the level beneath Color in the substructure of your gates. Your exact birth time determines your Tone, and Tone determines whether each arrow points LEFT or RIGHT.
